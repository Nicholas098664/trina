FAQs - FarmHub

What is FarmHub?

FarmHub is an online platform connecting farmers with buyers to sell fresh produce directly.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling in your details.

What products can I find on FarmHub?

FarmHub offers fresh fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy products, and more directly from farmers.

How can I place an order?

Browse the products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout to place your order.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and bank transfers.
